How Points Work

Predict the correct result (win, draw or loss) and earn 1 point — Get In! Get it wrong and earn nothing — Bad Shout! Each gameweek has 10 Premier League fixtures, so a perfect week scores 10 points.

Top Dog & Low Rider

The player with the most points at the end of each gameweek earns the title of This Week's Top Dog. The player with the fewest points gets the wooden spoon as This Week's Low Rider.
