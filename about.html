About OFP

Only Fools Predictions — The free Premier League prediction league on YouTube

What is OFP?

Only Fools Predictions (OFP) is a free Premier League prediction league that runs on YouTube. Every gameweek, we publish a predictions video on our YouTube channel, and viewers compete by submitting their own score predictions via a free entry form.

Results are tracked, points are awarded for correct results, and the leaderboard updates each week. At the end of each gameweek, the top scorer earns the title of Top Dog and the bottom scorer gets the wooden spoon as Low Rider.

It's completely free to enter. No registration, no subscription, no catches. Just Premier League football and friendly competition.

Top Dog & Low Rider

Each gameweek, the player with the highest points total earns the title of This Week's Top Dog — the gameweek winner, top of the pile.

The player with the fewest correct predictions that week gets the wooden spoon as This Week's Low Rider. Nobody wants that one. Both titles reset every gameweek so anyone can be Top Dog or Low Rider at any time.

FAQ

Is it really free?
Yes, completely free. No account needed, no subscription. Just subscribe to our YouTube channel and submit your predictions via the weekly Google Form.
When does the entry form open each week?
The form goes live when the predictions video is published — usually Thursday or Friday. The deadline is always before the first kick-off of the gameweek.
Do I have to predict score lines or just the result?
Just the result — home win, away win or draw. One point for each correct result. Simple.
How do I know what gameweek is open for predictions?
Check the Enter Free page — it always shows the current open gameweek and fixtures. You can also watch the latest video on our YouTube channel.
What happens at the end of the season?
The player with the most total points at the end of all 38 gameweeks wins Season One. We will announce prizes and end-of-season plans as the channel grows.
Will you add other leagues?
Season One is Premier League only. From Season Two we plan to add the Championship. Midweek fixtures may be covered separately under the EDP — Early Doors Predictions — format.
How can I support OFP?
Subscribe to the YouTube channel, share the videos, and if you want to support us financially you can buy us a coffee. Every bit helps keep the channel going.
